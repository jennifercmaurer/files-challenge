Rory Gilmore

Rory Gilmore grew up in the small town of Stars Hollow, Connecticut with her single mother, Lorelei Gilmore. Rory is very close to her mother. However, she does not see her father, Christopher very often. As a child, Rory also rarely saw her grandparents, but since beginning school at Chilton Prep, she began seeing her grandparents at a weekly Friday Night Dinner. Rory is a very intelligent who graduated high school as valedictorian with a 4.2 GPA. She went on to attend Yale University studying journalism. Rory loves to read. She shares her mothers love of coffee, junkfood, and movies. Rory Gilmore is the main character of Gilmore Girls.
